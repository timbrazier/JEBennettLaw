About Me

I am Jane Bennett, a solicitor qualified in England and Wales.

I have over 14 years’ experience working with Private Clients and over the last 7 years, have built up a practice which focuses on incapacitated clients through the protection of their assets, and the safeguarding of their personal welfare.

I have 7 years’ experience working in medium-sized City and Westminster law firms, and have also worked in a small Hong Kong-based law firm.

Our Work

Our work focuses on the protection of the vulnerable client. Most of our clients have an acquired brain injury and/or physical injuries sustained through an accident; disease; birth injuries, or infirmity.

We are primarily concerned with ensuring our clients can access the advice they need at any given time. Where we are not in a position to provide the advice ourselves such as in the case of a divorce/family law matter, or an employment issue, we ensure that they are referred to someone who can advise them appropriately.
